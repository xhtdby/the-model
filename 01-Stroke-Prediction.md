01 Stroke Prediction
Context
According to the World Health Organization (WHO) stroke is the 2nd leading cause of death
globally, responsible for approximately 11% of total deaths. Here you are asked to predict
whether a patient is likely to get stroke based on patient clinical data and lab results.
Related publications

Stroke Prediction." 2020 4th International Conference on Electronics, Communication and
